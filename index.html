Visionary Decision Making

Exerpts on how to Cultivate Intuitive Intelligence

The Visionary Decision Making (VDM) process outlined in this book relies upon execution of these essential skills:

How to notice and leverage synchronicities. Synchronicities offer a unique opportunity to receive information from outside the box of linear thinking by drawing from the realm of Infinite Intelligence.

How to tune into our collective resource of Infinite Intelligence. Infinite Intelligence surrounds and is available to everyone; it is the origin of all great ideas and the source of all creative powers.

How to develop and finetune your intuitive sense. Mindfulness practices create space for intuition to operate freely by turning down the noise level of the other senses, allowing for better reception of subtle insights and synchronistic signals.

How to discover your most creative self. Be motivated by what fascinates you, your aptitudes and natural temperaments. Understand your true nature and get acquainted with your gut.

How to master strategic thinking and visionary resources. Harness archetypes and the Chinese Book of Changes as intuitive decision-making aids. Learn how to recruit mentors, counselors and guides. Employ logic to your strategic advantage while letting intuition be your guide.

How to upgrade your belief system. Be clear about and take ownership of your beliefs and operating assumptions. Trust yourself first and put them to the test. Adopt beliefs that make sense to you, because your attitude, decisions and actions depend on them.

How to execute the decisions at the right time. Perseverance furthers and mastery is a reward. Like compound interest, even slight better decisions add up to incredible benefit over time.

The process of VDM and the creation of a “synchronistic lifestyle” are profoundly simple, accessible to everyone, practical and easy to implement. It begins with awareness and ends with action. A positive outlook, faith in your purpose and destiny supported by a cultivated intuition will help you ensure that Great Decisions and Perfect Timing become the standard operating procedure of how you conduct your life.

Paul O'Brien

Paul O’Brien founded what became the world’s largest astrology and divination website and is known as the father of interactive divination. He is a sought-after visionary, entrepreneur and speaker. He is the author of The Visionary I Ching: A Book of Changes for the 21st Century (ebook and app) and the book Divination: Sacred Tools for Reading the Mind of God and he serves as Executive Director of the Divination Foundation (Divination.com). For 30 years he has hosted the Pathways radio show, an interview program focused on personal and cultural transformation.
