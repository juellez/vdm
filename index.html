Excerpts on how to Cultivate Intuitive Intelligence

The Visionary Decision Making (VDM) process outlined in the book relies upon the development of special skills:

How to not only notice synchronicities, but leverage them. Synchronicities offer a unique opportunity to receive information from a realm of Infinite Intelligence.

How to tune into our collective resource of "Infinite Intelligence." Infinite Intelligence envelopes us and is available to everyone; it is the origin of all great ideas and a resource of creative powers.

How to finetune your intuitive antenna. Intuitive mindfulness creates space for intuition to operate freely—by turning down the noise level of the other senses, allowing for better reception of subtle insights and synchronistic signals.

How to discover your most creative self. Focus on what fascinates you, that which engages your aptitudes and natural temperament. Understand your true nature and get aligned with your personal destiny.

How to master strategic thinking and tap creative resources. Harness archetypes and the I Ching as intuitive decision-making aids. Learn how to recruit mentors, counselors and guides. Employ logic to your strategic advantage while letting intuition creatively guide the way.

How to upgrade your beliefs. Be clear about and take ownership of your current operating assumptions. Be willing to put them to the test. Adopt only the beliefs that make sense to you—because your attitude, decisions and actions will depend on them.

How to execute decisions in order to make the right moves at the right time. Perseverance furthers as you develop mastery. Like compound interest, even slightly better decisions will add up to incredible benefit over time.

Visionary decision making and the development of a “synchronistic lifestyle” are potentials that are accessible to everyone, highly idealistic but eminently practical, and easy to implement. It begins with awareness and ends with action. A positive outlook, faith in your purpose supported by an activated intuition ensures that Great Decisions and Perfect Timing become standard operating procedure in your life.

Paul O'Brien

Paul O’Brien founded what became the world’s largest astrology and divination website and became known as "the father of interactive divination" for his invention of this category of software in 1989. He is a sought-after visionary entrepreneur, speaker and advisor. He is the author of The Visionary I Ching: A Book of Changes for the 21st Century (ebook and app) and the book Divination: Sacred Tools for Reading the Mind of God and he serves as Executive Director of the Divination Foundation (Divination.com). For 30 years he has hosted the Pathways radio show, an interview program focused on personal and cultural transformation.
